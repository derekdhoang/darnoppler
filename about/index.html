MEET DARNELL

Darnell is a passionate weather enthusiast and community broadcaster serving Eastern Iowa. What started as a hobby tracking storms has grown into a trusted source of weather information for over 200 followers on Facebook.

With a deep understanding of Iowa's unique weather patterns — from severe thunderstorms and tornadoes in spring to blizzards in winter — Darnell brings accurate, timely forecasts directly to the community through engaging Facebook Live broadcasts and daily updates.

THE MISSION

Tracking storms. Protecting people.

Our mission is simple: provide Iowans with the most accurate, accessible, and timely weather information possible. We believe everyone deserves to know when severe weather threatens their home, workplace, or community.

Darnoppler's Forecast combines professional-grade weather data from NOAA and the Storm Prediction Center with local expertise and community-first broadcasting. Whether it's a tornado warning or the week's outlook, we're here to keep Iowa informed and safe.
